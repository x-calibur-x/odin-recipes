Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish made with a thin crust topped with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. Its simple ingredients create a perfect balance of tangy, creamy, and fresh flavors.

Known for its elegance, this pizza highlights the quality of each ingredient, with a lightly crisp crust and smooth, melted cheese in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • ¼ cup flour for dusting
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste
